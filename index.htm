Global equity, global politics, local success: Introducing

Bitcoin 1776 and the World Series of Bitcoin

Bitcoin 1776 is new version of the Bitcoin Core software, forking July 4th, 2018. The maximum number of coins 17.76 million. This increases final equity from 82% to 96% for all Bitcoin holders. Our blocks are found every 10 minutes (on average), with a halving every 210,000. Payout is 0.25 JFK to miners, representing part of one Kennedys Coin.

Our social structure is the World Series of Bitcoin. Teams of 8 elect 2 representatives, who move on to higher levels of councilors. If you are interested in participating, download our software and say high on Reddit! Our first contests will be starting soon.
